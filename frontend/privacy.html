Privacy Policy

At CivicFix, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, submit a report, or contact us for support:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Content of reports you submit (photos, descriptions, location data)
  • Communications with CivicFix

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and track reports you submit
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage

Sharing of Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With local authorities to address reported issues
  • With service providers who need access to perform work on our behalf
  • If required to do so by law or in response to legal process
  • To protect the rights and property of CivicFix and our users

Location Data

When you submit a report, we collect precise location data from your device to accurately identify the issue location. This data is essential for our service and is shared with relevant authorities to address the reported problem.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy.

Your Choices

You can update your account information at any time by accessing your account settings. You may also delete your account, which will remove your personal information from our active databases.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@civicfix.com.
