Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive

How do I create a CivicFix account?

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page. You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password. Alternatively, you can sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.

Why is my location not being detected correctly?

Location detection issues are usually caused by browser or device settings. Ensure that:

  • Your browser has permission to access your location
  • Location services are enabled on your device
  • You have a stable internet connection
  • You're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)

If problems persist, you can manually set your location on the map when reporting an issue.

How long does it take for reported issues to be resolved?

Resolution times vary depending on the type of issue and your local authorities. Simple issues like potholes or broken streetlights are typically addressed within 1-2 weeks. More complex issues may take longer. You can track the status of your reports in your account dashboard.

How does the points system work?

You earn points for various activities on CivicFix:

  • 5 points for each verified issue report
  • 2 points for each helpful comment on other reports
  • 10 points when your reported issue gets resolved
  • Bonus points for reporting urgent or hazardous issues

Climb the leaderboard to become a top contributor in your community!

Can I report issues anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to report issues anonymously. However, anonymous reports do not earn points or contribute to your leaderboard standing. Some local authorities may also be less responsive to anonymous reports as they cannot follow up for additional information if needed.

What types of issues can I report on CivicFix?

You can report various community issues including:

  • Potholes and road damage
  • Broken streetlights
  • Garbage accumulation and illegal dumping
  • Park maintenance issues
  • Sewer and drainage problems
  • Graffiti and vandalism
  • Traffic sign issues
  • Other public infrastructure problems
